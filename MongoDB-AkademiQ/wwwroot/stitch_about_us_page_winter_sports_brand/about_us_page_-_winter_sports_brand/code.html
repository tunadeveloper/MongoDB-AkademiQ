From the Garage to the Summit

It started with a simple idea: ski gear shouldn't just survive the cold; it should thrive in it. Founded by two professional snowboarders who were tired of equipment failing in extreme conditions, SnowPeak began as a passion project.

We spent years testing prototypes on the harshest peaks we could find. If a zipper froze or a seam burst, we started over. That obsession with failure is what led to our success.

Today, we are still rider-owned and driven by the same promise: no compromises. Just pure, unadulterated performance for those who hear the mountain calling.
