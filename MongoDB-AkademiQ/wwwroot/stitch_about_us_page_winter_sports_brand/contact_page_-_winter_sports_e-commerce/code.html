Frequently Asked Questions

Save time by checking if your question is answered here.

How do I track my order? expand_more

Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking link. You can also view your order status by logging into your account dashboard.

What is your return policy for ski equipment? expand_more

We accept returns on unused equipment within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in original packaging. Custom-mounted skis are final sale.

Do you offer international shipping? expand_more

Yes, we ship to most countries with winter sports destinations. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location and are calculated at checkout.
